My awakening with matrescence after the birth of my wonderful babies led me here. My motherhood journey took a shift in the most beautiful way and allowed me to help moms in their own.
As moms, we need not prove that we can do it all by ourselves. Instead, we can honor and allow ourselves to thrive and be nurtured by community.

MATRESCENCE, LIKE ADOLESCENCE
This is a time in a woman's life when her whole identity changes. Motherhood splits her into two: the woman she used to be, and the mom she is now

My Story

I always looked forward to the day I would become a mom- the praise and admiration just like I admired my grandmothers, aunts and my mother. After my daughter's birth, my reality came in. I was struggling and felt like I could not find a way to express it. It was beautiful, don't get me wrong, to watch the creation that was once in my body in my arms but there was something missing. I did not know what it was yet.
When I discovered matrescence, I finally began to understand what a mother goes through; that a mother is born and experiences changes just like the baby. I wasn't crazy after all. This led me to a series of reaching out to other moms and sharing my own naked truths. It was hard to be vulnerable but deep down I knew that a real woman's strength was telling her stories without fear of judgment. It's been two years since my awakening, and I am excited to share this space with you.
