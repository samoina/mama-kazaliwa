frequently asked questions

Q. What does Mama Kazaliwa do? Please explain more.
Mama Kazaliwa offers support and resources for mothers as they make the transition to motherhood. This is made possible in three ways: through the Mama Kazaliwa Community, our postpartum packages and our gatherings.

Q. What is matrescence?
Matrescence is the radical identity shift a woman goes through as she becomes a mother. Dr. Aurelie Athan explains that matrescence is just like adolescence. This change manifests biologically, psychologically, socially, emotionall even spiritually and has been likened to the remarkable developments that occur during adolescence. As such, Mama Kazaliwa places mothers at the heart of what we do. Read more about matrescence here

Q. How can I get in touch with Mama Kazaliwa?
Mama Kazaliwa has a vibrant Instagram page ( @mama_kazaliwa) through which we seek to educate and empower moms. You can send us a Direct Message or simply use our contact form below and we will get back in touch.

Q. Does Mama Kazaliwa have packages available?
Mama Kazaliwa offers postpartum packages through which we support newly postpartum mommas as they adjust to life with a newborn. These packages may be offered virtually or in-person with our home visits option.

Q. Is Mama Kazaliwa only for moms who are newly postpartum?
Certainly not! While our postpartum package caters to new moms, our community and monthly gatherings are open for just about every momma. Every stage of motherhood has its share of challenges and wins, and we want to be with you as you go through these moments.

Q. Is this a substitute for therapy?
Thank you for asking! Mama Kazaliwa exists to inform, educate and support mommas through their matrescence journey. For this reason, the information provided here is meant to educate and is NOT a replacement for professional medical attention. Consequently, it is not to be used to diagnose or treat any health condition. Please seek professional health services from qualified medical personnel.

Q. How can I support Mama Kazaliwa?
First, thank you for considering to support the work that we do. You can shop our affirmation cards here, contribute to the Mama Kazaliwa period project, offer your skills at our monthly gatherings or even make a cash donation.

Q. Where are you located?
Mama Kazaliwa is based in Kikuyu town, with our services available across the country.
