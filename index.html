Journey to Matrescence

I remember vividly when I discovered I was pregnant in 2016. I had mixed feelings: extremely scared but very excited about what the new chapter in my life would hold. On the the day my daughter came, I was so scared of how my body would handle the pain and soon after that my reality came in. I was struggling - all these big feelings started trickling in.
Shame, guilt, doubt - I could not find a way to express what it was. It was beautiful but there was something missing and I did not know what it was yet. When I found out about matrescence, It was like the aha! moment... Read More

Mama Kazaliwa Community

Motherhood is natural, not automatic.
Motherhood looks different for each and every mom. Mama Kazaliwa is here to support women during this transition. Simply put, Matrescence is the process of becoming a mother, and it embodies the significant physical, emotional and psychological changes. Mama Kazaliwa is here to support you as you navigate this identity change, meeting you where you are with grace and kindness.
